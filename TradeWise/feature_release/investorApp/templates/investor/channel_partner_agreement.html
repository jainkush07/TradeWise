  1. AGREEMENT TERMS

The Channel Partner agreement is valid for a period of 99 years, commencing as of {{agreement_date}}, to {{agreement_end_date}}.

1.        Referred Buyer/ Investor

  • A Buyer/Investor shall qualify as a Buyer/investor if investor has agreed to invest in the same security within the security offers by Planify or investor reached to the Channel Partner with the reference to Planify.
  • A Referred Buyer shall also include any affiliate, subsidiary, or related parties under common control of such Referred Buyer as well as prospective Buyers referred by existing Referred Buyers.
  • The territory for purposes of this Agreement with respect to financial product and service shall be across the globe for investor.
  •  The Channel Partner will get the commission percentage for the clients which will purchase the product or avail the services through him/her.
  •  The Channel Partner will be entitled to any commissions on any further investments made by the Referred Buyer/Investor with Planify.

 

  1. PLANIFY COMMITMENTS

a.        Commissions

  • The Planify shall pay the Agent a commission of 1% to 10% of the selling/buying price or total business for each order and deal duly remitted by the Agent in accordance with this Agreement.
  • Monthly Distribution of commission shall be made with-in 11 days of each month.
  • Planify holds the right to map the referred buyer, brought by any other channel partner to the Channel Partner.

b.        Business Support

  • Channel Partners, further associating with Planify from the pin code city of Channel Partner will be a part of the Channel Partner team.
  • Planify agrees to provide Channel Partner with prospects, that are willing to meet, from pin code city of Channel Partner for further perusal, if any.

c.        Marketing Support

  • Planify agrees to provide Daily prices of securities, research reports, and other marketing/advertising material to Channel Partner.
  • Planify would be promoting/advertising its Channel Partner through various social media channels.
  • Planify  would  publish Channel Partner’s  name        with        contact        details        of Channel Partner on its website and YouTube channel.

 

  1. CHANNEL PARTNER COMMITMENTS

a.        Marketing:

  • With respect to advertising of any financial product or services related to Planify, Channel Partner agrees to consult with Planify and to keep Planify reasonably appraised of its marketing plans and activities.
  • Planify shall exercise its approval rights in a timely and reasonable manner. Channel Partner is required to place one marketing billboard presenting Planify, the design for which will be given by Planify, outside as well as inside of his/her office, the expenses for which will be borne by Channel Partner.

b.        A Buyer shall be disqualified as a Referred Buyer if, within two business days of a Referral, the Channel Partner can provide substantive, written evidence that the Channel Partner had, previously and independently from the Planify efforts, contacted the Buyer regarding a Transaction for the Securities.

Channel Partner and Planify hereby acknowledge and agree that all information contained in, relating to, or furnished pursuant to this Agreement, not otherwise known to the public, is confidential and proprietary and is not to be disclosed to third parties without the prior written consent of both Channel Partner and Planify. Neither Channel Partner nor Planify shall disclose such information to any third party (other than to officers, directors, employees, attorneys, accountants and agents of Channel Partner and Planify or the affiliates of either, who have a business reason to know or have access to such information, and only after each of whom agrees to being bound by this paragraph).

  1. AUDIT RIGHTS

a.        During the Term and continuing until 1 month following the date of expiration or earlier termination of this Agreement Planify may, audit the financial books/D-MAT accounts/shares related transactions, information systems and records of Channel Partner as reasonably necessary to verify Channel Partner's compliance with its obligations under this Agreement.

b.        Planify may not audit more than twice per year (and no such audit shall continue for more than 20 calendar days from the date the auditors are given access to the applicable records).

c.        Any such audit shall be conducted only during regular business hours and in such a manner as not unreasonably to interfere with the normal business activities of Channel Partner.
